WHAT DO SEA TURTLES EAT?

The meat eaters eat crabs,mollusks shrimp and jellyfish

The plant eaters eat seagrasses (seaweed) and algae

Sea turtles often mistakes plastic bags for jellyfish and suffocate themselves

Whats is our purpose

Protect

We have a big team that is growing up every day andchecks to every beach in Ionian sea that has protected areas for sea turtles.You can also join us if you want.Just send us an email and we will gladly put you on a patrol.

Recycle

Recycling garbage is one of our main purposes daily .We are gathering all king of garbage that can be recycled and take them to the nearest special recycling bin.Also we use patrols to inform people at the beach about it.
